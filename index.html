Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Fill out the online or offline application form. Ensure all required documents are attached.

Step 2: Submission

Submit the completed application along with the necessary documents to our admission office.

Step 3: Evaluation

Our admission committee reviews and considering academic performance, extracurricular activities, and other criteria.

Step 4: Notification

Once the evaluation is complete, we will notify you about the admission decision.You will receive an acceptance letter.

Step 5: Enrollment

To confirm your enrollment, you will need to pay the required fees and complete the enrollment process.
